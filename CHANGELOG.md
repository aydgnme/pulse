# Changelog

## 1.0.0 — 2026-07-03

First release.

- One-tap timing gameplay: tap when the needle crosses the target; every hit
  reverses direction, raises speed, and shrinks the window
- PERFECT hits (dead-center) worth double, with chains
- Pressure spawns: occasional close targets from score 5
- Needle colour drifts mint → amber as speed climbs
- Near-miss death notes ("MISSED BY 3°", "TOO SLOW")
- Pause (button + auto-pause on backgrounding), menu, instant retry
- Score sharing via the native share sheet
- Original synthesized looping soundtrack with persistent mute toggle
- Best score persisted on-device; no ads, no tracking, fully offline
