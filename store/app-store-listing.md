## Description (EN)

```
One tap is all you get.

A glowing needle orbits the ring. Tap the moment it crosses the target —
then do it again, and again, as every hit reverses the direction, raises
the speed, and shrinks your timing window. Tap too early, too late, or
let the needle sail past, and the run is over.

Pulse is pure timing. No lives, no power-ups, no menus between you and
the next attempt. Just you, the ring, and a score to beat.

HOW TO PLAY
• Tap when the needle overlaps the target dot
• Every hit flips the direction and speeds things up
• One wrong tap — or one missed target — ends the run
• Tap again to instantly retry

WHY YOU'LL KEEP COMING BACK
• One-thumb play, sessions as short as ten seconds
• A difficulty curve that ramps every single hit
• Crisp haptic feedback on every hit and every miss
• Your best score is always on screen, daring you
• Minimal neon design, zero clutter
• Fully offline — no account, no ads, no data collected

Easy to learn. Brutal to master. How far can you push your streak?
```

## App Privacy (App Store Connect questionnaire)

- **Data collection: "Data Not Collected."** The app stores the best score
  on-device with AsyncStorage; nothing leaves the phone. No analytics, no
  ads, no third-party SDKs, no account.
- Privacy policy URL: host `store/privacy-policy.md` somewhere public
  (GitHub repo page works) and paste the URL.
