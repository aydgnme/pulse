# Pulse — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 3, 2026_

Pulse does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

- **No data leaves your device.** Your best score is saved locally on your
  phone and nowhere else. Deleting the app deletes it.
- **No account** is required or offered.
- **No analytics, no tracking, no advertising** SDKs are included.
- **No network access** is used by the game.

Because the app collects no data, there is nothing for us to sell, share, or
lose. If this policy ever changes, this document will be updated before any
change takes effect.
